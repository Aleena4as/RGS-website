THE GUILDFORD HERITAGE

Excellence Since 1509

We combine 500 years of educational excellence with a mindset for leadership and a devotion to wellbeing.

Royal Grammar School Guildford Dubai reflects the same high standards as our parent school in the UK, one of the most prestigious independent British Curriculum schools in the country.

- Consistently ranked as one of the leading schools in the UK at both A Level and GCSE according to the national league tables
- More than 325 graduates have gone on to attend top universities such as Oxford and Cambridge over the past 10 years
- Alumni include a number of Olympic and sporting personalities, a former Prime Minister, and many notable names in politics and the arts

Where a foundation of tradition, a preparation for the future and a lifelong love of learning are coming together under one beautiful roof.
